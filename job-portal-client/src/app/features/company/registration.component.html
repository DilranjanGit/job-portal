Company Registration

Company name is required. Name must be at least 2 characters.
Email is required. Enter a valid email.
Location is required. Location must be at least 2 characters.
Website is required. Website must be a valid URL (e.g., https://www.example.com).
Password is required. Minimum 6 characters. Password must contain at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one special character, and one number.
